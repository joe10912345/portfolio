Speeder Pro Earbuds

Earbuds
Speeder Pro
Material & CMF Exploration

The Speeder Pro is a high-performance gaming earphone equipped with advanced high-speed connection technology, ensuring a seamless gaming experience with zero sound delay. Its design features a frosted glass panel as the centerpiece, complemented by a reflective bite-effect logo beneath it. The product aims to strike a balance between subtle sophistication and an aggressive, bold aesthetic.
