Laptop
SAGITTARIUS
Stay True to Yourself &middot; Unique Personality
Presented with a minimalist design language, the laptop features CNC machining and anodized finishing, complemented by delicate texturing for a distinctive, premium feel. Every detail is carefully crafted, from the A cover to the D cover, and even the hinge, reflecting an uncompromising pursuit of refinement.
This is more than just a laptop. Its unique blue-gray finish with platinum accents embodies an attitude—staying true to yourself, exuding professionalism and individuality, and empowering you to stand out in both work and life.

Tranquil Blue-Gray & Platinum
Designed for the urban elite who pursue lifestyle quality and unique individuality. The multi-layered blue-gray tones break away from the stereotypes of traditional business equipment, complemented by platinum-colored details that evoke a sense of calm, confident modern charm. It seamlessly blends work and leisure, highlighting the user's unconventional and discerning taste.

Obsidian Black & Matte Gold
Crafted specifically for the composed and sophisticated executive. The deep obsidian black chassis exudes a subtle yet premium metallic texture, while the refined gold accents on the hinge perfectly embody an aura of prestige and grandeur. With every fold and opening, it radiates impeccable professionalism and decisive leadership.
