Gemtek ID Family
Geometry Series
Designed with Order. Built for Modern Living.
As Gemtek's second complete product family after the Ocean Series, the Geometry Series extends our design exploration into the 5G and Coax product lines.
With the experience gained from developing a unified family language, we turned to architecture and contemporary interiors as the foundation for this series. Through careful studies of form, proportion, surface rhythm, material contrast, and refined detailing, we aimed to create products that feel composed, sophisticated, and naturally integrated into modern homes.
The result is a family of connectivity devices that no longer feels like background equipment, but like considered objects&mdash;quietly present, visually refined, and worth noticing in everyday life.
