Earbuds
TEANA
Material & CMF Exploration
The Teana earbuds feature a simple yet luxurious design, targeting women aged 25&ndash;35. For its CMF (Color, Material, Finish), Morandi colors&mdash;known for their trendy and versatile appeal&mdash;were selected, making it easy to pair with various outfits. The speaker hole is crafted from stainless steel for durability and aesthetic quality. In terms of ergonomics, a side hook was added to the shell to ensure a secure fit within the ear.
To stand out, the design evolved into four distinct variations, each defined by unique 3D textures on the front panel, such as diamond patterns, concave surfaces, and wave features. Additionally, the material was upgraded to recycled plastic, reinforcing the product's sustainability and modern appeal.
