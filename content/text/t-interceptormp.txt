T. Interceptor MP
Autonomous AI-powered drone, guarding airspace with speed and agility.
As drone threats grow more prevalent, T.Interceptor MP is a multi-functional defense system built for urban security and critical infrastructure protection. It integrates AI image recognition, modular design, and cloud communication to perform real-time detection, monitoring, and interception in complex environments.
Powered by AI flight control and visual recognition, the system autonomously detects and locks onto suspicious aerial targets without continuous human oversight. Once confirmed, it launches a physical capture net to intercept the threat and bring it safely to the ground &mdash; delivering high-mobility protection with minimal impact on the surrounding environment.

Reconnaissance, interception, and rapid response &mdash; one platform, multiple missions.
Autonomous Detection, Tracking & Interception
No need for personnel with drone jamming guns on-site. The T.Interceptor MP intercepts threats autonomously, while net-based capture minimizes collateral impact to the surrounding environment.
Form Follows Function
The T. Interceptor MP breaks from the utilitarian bulk of conventional defense platforms. Its compact, muscular silhouette communicates readiness and versatility &mdash; a form shaped equally for high-speed pursuit and steady reconnaissance. Every surface is resolved with intent, projecting capability without unnecessary visual complexity.
Flight Testing & Validation
From controlled indoor environments to open-field scenarios, each flight test validates the system's autonomous detection, tracking, and net-based capture performance. Iterative testing under real-world conditions ensures reliability across diverse operational environments &mdash; refining flight dynamics, sensor accuracy, and structural durability before deployment.
The rear-mounted battery module is designed for rapid field replacement &mdash; minimizing downtime between missions. The tool-free extraction mechanism allows operators to swap power units in seconds, keeping the system mission-ready with minimal interruption.
Multi-Sensor Gimbal System
Equipped with a high-performance stabilized gimbal integrating visible light, laser rangefinder, and thermal imaging &mdash; enabling all-weather target identification, distance measurement, and tracking across day and night operations.
Angled Net Launcher &mdash; CG-Aligned Design
The capture net launcher is mounted at a calculated angle rather than horizontal. This ensures the reaction force during net deployment passes through the drone's center of gravity, preventing destabilizing torque that could cause mid-flight rollover. The angled geometry is seamlessly integrated into the body's surface language, turning an engineering constraint into a defining design detail.
