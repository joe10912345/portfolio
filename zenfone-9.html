Zenfone 9

A smartphone CMF concept inspired by stage lighting, performance, and emotional contrast.

Zenfone 9 CMF Concept explores how color, light, and surface finish can create a stronger emotional identity for a compact smartphone. Inspired by the atmosphere of live performances, the design transforms shifting stage lights, backstage shadows, and energetic visual rhythm into a refined color and material strategy.
